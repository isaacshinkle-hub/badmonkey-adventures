Copper Canyon
6-Day Flagship

This is the bread-and-butter trip — already dialed. Start and finish in Bisbee, Arizona, then ride through Banámichi, into Copper Canyon country, through Batopilas and Urique, and back. Up to 5 guest riders + guide.

The Loop

Bisbee → Canyon Country → Bisbee

A guided adventure motorcycle expedition built around big landscapes, small roads, border-town culture, and canyon descents that earn every mile.

Group Size

Up to 5 guest riders plus Isaac. Not a parade — a tight crew that can adapt when the road gets interesting.

6 Days

Long enough for the full Copper Canyon loop feel. Short enough to fit real life and shoulder seasons.

Dialed Route

Primary path: Bisbee → Banámichi → Copper Canyon → Batopilas → Urique → Bisbee.
