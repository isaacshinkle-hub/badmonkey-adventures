Couple Passages
Aboard SV Sea Monkey

Because of boat size, we take only 2 guests at a time — perfect for a relationship couple or two close friends. Join the boat for generally 2–3 week passages while Sea Monkey bumps around the world. Isaac sails with one or two crew in addition to the paid guests.

The Model

Shared Expenses, Real Passages

This is not a mega-yacht charter. Guests contribute toward boat operation and maintenance on a shared-expense structure — usually about $30–$50 per person per day depending on route and season.

2 Guests Only

Intimate by design. Book as a couple or as two friends who travel well together.

2–3 Week Passages

Long enough to settle into boat life, stand watches, and actually go somewhere.

Crewed Adventure

Isaac plus 1–2 crew handle the serious boat work while guests plug in as much as they want.
